The Phone that started it all...

The original iPhone, launched by Apple in 2007, revolutionized the mobile industry with its sleek design and advanced features. With a 3.5-inch capacitive touchscreen and a minimalist appearance, it boasted a high-quality display and introduced the concept of Multi-Touch for intuitive gestures. Powered by the iOS operating system, it provided a user-friendly interface and access to a growing number of applications through the App Store. Connectivity options included 2G (EDGE) and Wi-Fi, while a rear 2-megapixel camera allowed for capturing photos and videos. The original iPhone set the stage for the modern smartphone era and paved the way for the numerous advancements in mobile technology that followed.
